  5. Orange peel can be reused to remove grease, oil spots and orange tea.

  7. In terms of the history of colors, it’s only recently that the color orange got its own name. Surprisingly, the fruit came first, originating in China, and the English word ‘orange’ to describe the color, followed thereafter.
  8. There are now over 600 varieties of oranges worldwide Oranges originates around 4000 BC in Southeast Asia, and then spread into India
  9. The flowers of an orange tree are white in color and have a wonderful fragrance
