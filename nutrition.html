Nutrition

Nutritional breakdown for 1 orange:

  • Calories: 66
  • Water: 86% by weight
  • Protein: 1.3 grams
  • Carbs: 14.8 grams
  • Sugar: 12 grams
  • Fiber: 2.8 grams
  • Fat: 0.2 grams
  • Vitamin C: 92% of the Daily Value (DV)
  • Folate: 9% of the DV
  • Calcium: 5% of the DV
  • Potassium: 5% of the DV

Like most fruits, oranges are mostly made up of carbs and water, doesn't contain much protein and fat, and are relatively low in calories.

Also are a good source of fiber!

Oranges are full of phenolic compounds — especially flavonoids, which contribute to most of their antioxidant properties.
