Garibaldi Recipe

Ingredients & Proportions

  • Campari: 1.5 oz (or 45 ml)
  • Orange Juice: 4 oz (or 120 ml), preferably freshly squeezed from high-pulp oranges (like Valencia)
  • Ice: Plenty of fresh cubes
  • Garnish: Orange wedge or half-wheel

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prep the Juice: Pour your fresh orange juice into a blender or use a handheld milk frother. Blend or froth on high for 10-15 seconds until the juice becomes light, airy, and frothy.
  2. Build the Drink: Fill a tall highball or rocks glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour: Pour the 1.5 oz of Campari directly over the ice.
  4. Top and Stir: Top with the frothed orange juice and gently stir to integrate the ingredients without completely flattening the foam.
  5. Garnish: Top the rim of the glass with your orange wedge or half-wheel.
